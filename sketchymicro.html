Videos

All the videos of SketchyMirco and SketchyPharm are included in their native quality. The videos are offered in 1080p, 720p, 480p and 360p resolutions. Please note that some of the videos are natively low quality regardless of their 1080p or even 720p badge.

Captions

The capions are included for the videos that came with them in vtt format. Please change the extension to txt for better compatibility.  And make sure that the video file as well as the subtitle file have the same name and are located in the same directory.  Then play the video with VLC media player.  You may want to put the subtitle file in a special “Subtitles” folder for certain apps, such as “PlayerXtreme” on iOS.

Review Images

The review images are included in their native quality in html format. So you can interact with the red spots.
